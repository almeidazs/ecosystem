# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AbacatePay is committed to providing an open, welcoming, safe, and respectful environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

This Code of Conduct applies to **all AbacatePay open-source repositories**, including this monorepo and **all packages published under the AbacatePay organization**, as well as all interactions related to these projects (Pull Requests, Issues, comments, code reviews, and related community spaces).

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

- Treat other community members with respect and empathy.
- Accept constructive criticism in a professional and mature manner.
- Collaborate in good faith, prioritizing the collective quality of the project.
- Foster an environment free from harassment, discrimination, or hostility.
- Maintain clear, objective, and professional communication.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Use of offensive, discriminatory, or sexualized language or imagery.
- Personal attacks or non-technical attacks unrelated to the discussion.
- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or derogatory comments of any kind.
- Unauthorized sharing of private or sensitive information.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional, collaborative open-source environment.

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in actions ranging from a warning to temporary or permanent removal from participation in AbacatePay projects.

Enforcement decisions are made by the project maintainers based on the severity and context of the violation.
Maintainers are not required to publicly **justify or disclose details** of enforcement actions.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Send an email to: *[security@abacatepay.com](mailto:security@abacatepay.com)*
- Include as much relevant detail as possible (links, screenshots, context)

All reports are handled with seriousness and confidentiality.

---

## References

This Code of Conduct is inspired by established best practices in the open-source community, including the Contributor Covenant.

For security-related concerns, please refer to our Security Policy.
